Dear Von Neumann,

We have here a very good young mathematician, Dr. Kurt S t r e b e l.  He is my assistent, but he is a pupil of Nevanlinna's. Dr. Strebel will shortly be admitted as Privatdozent. Now, both Prof. Nevanlinna and myself thought it would be very good for Dr. Strebel to spend a year in the United States. Would it be possible to obtain a fellowship for Princeton, beginning autumn 1953? I enclose a Gutachten on his Habilitationsschrift, from which you can see what his work is about. His previous publications are:

Eine Bemerkung zur Hebbarkeit des Randes einer Riemannschen Fläche.
Comm. math. Helv. 1949.
Eine Ungleichung für extremale Längen.
Ann. Acad. Sci. Fenn. 1951.
Ueber das Kreisnormierungsproblem der konformen Abbildung.
Ann. Acad. Sci. Fenn. 1951.

Dr. Strebel is Swiss, a very nice and jolly fellow, and an excellent teacher.

Would you mind sending me an application form, that is, if you judge that Dr. Strebel's chances are good?

Yours most cordially
B

(Prof. Dr. B. L. van der Waerden)
