PRINCETON, NEW JERSEY
Dec. 5.
Dear Mr. Alexanderoff, Many thanks for the kind invitation to send you excerpts of my Moscow lectures. It so happens that, as early as May 1935, I completed a manuscript on my congress lecture, which I intended to publish in the “Annals of Mathematics” under the title “The Unicity of Haar’ Measure.” Its length is the maximum you specified: about 15 standard printed pages. I would therefore like to
