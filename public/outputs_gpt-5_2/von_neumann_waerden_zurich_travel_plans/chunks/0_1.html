Professor B. L. van der Waerden Mathematisches Institut der Universität Zürich Zürich, Switzerland Dear van der Waerden: Please excuse my delay in answering your very kind invitation of July 13--I have been travelling a good deal and was in Mexico during most of the last five weeks. I would like to visit Zürich during my European trip next summer, but I am afraid that I won't be able to come there after the Congress. I expect that I will have to return then more or less directly (with at most a short stopover in England) to Princeton. I do expect to come to Zürich shortly before the Congress--but I am afraid that this may not be a very good time for seeing you or the other colleagues there or to give a talk, since this is likely to be your vacation period. Am I correct in this? If this is not the case, I would be more than delighted to stop in Zürich before the Congress and give a talk at this time, if this is agreeable to you.
