Hs 652: 6529
July 13, 1953
Professor J.  von Neumann Institute for Advanced Study Princeton N.J.,  U.S.A.
My dear Prince,
You would give us great pleasure if, after the Amsterdam Congress, preferably in the last week of October, you would give a lecture at the joint colloquium of the University and the E.T.H. Zurich. We regret that we cannot offer you a princely honorarium, but 300 Swiss francs are available for your travel expenses. The invitation comes from Finsler, Nevanlinna and myself, but the gentlemen from the E.T.H. would also be very pleased if you would accept. The details we can then settle in the summer of 1954.
Kind regards Yours


(B. L. van der Waerden)
