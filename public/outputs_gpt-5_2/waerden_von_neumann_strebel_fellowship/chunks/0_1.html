Dear Von Neumann,
        We have here a very good young mathematician, Dr. Kurt
S t r e b e l.  He is my assistent, but he is a pupil of
Nevanlinna's.  Dr. Strebel will shortly be admitted as Privatdozent.
Now, both Prof. Nevanlinna and myself thought it would be very
good for Dr. Strebel to spend a year in the United States.  Would
it be possible to obtain a fellowship for Princeton, beginning
autumn 1953?  I enclose a Gutachten on his Habilitationsschrift,
from which you can see what his work is about.  His previous publi-
cations are:

        Eine Bemerkung zur Hebbarkeit des Randes einer
        Riemannschen Fläche.  Comm.math.Helv. 1949.

        Eine Ungleichung für extremale Längen.
        Ann. Acad.Sci. Fenn. 1951.

        Ueber das Kreisnormierungsproblem der konformen
        Abbildung.           Ann.Acad.Sci. Fenn.  1951.
