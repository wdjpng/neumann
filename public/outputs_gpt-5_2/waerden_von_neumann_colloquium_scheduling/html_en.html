Professor Dr. J. von Neumann
Institute of Advanced Study
Princeton

Dear von Neumann, You are right that August-September is a vacation period here. The vacations begin about July 18. I am very sorry that a talk at our Colloquium therefore is out of question. However, I shall be delighted to see you personally. I shall be in town from August 20th until the beginning of the Amsterdam congress.
Yours most cordially (B.L. van der Waerden)
